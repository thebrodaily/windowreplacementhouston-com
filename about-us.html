Experience From Every Angle

We're not just installers. We're a team of career window professionals who came together to create a better experience for Houston homeowners.

Founded by Insiders to Fix a Broken Industry.

Window Replacement Houston was founded by a group of industry veterans with a shared frustration. After spending over 20 years in every part of the window business—from the factory floor where windows are made, to in-home sales and complex installations—we saw firsthand how the industry was failing homeowners.

We saw companies pushing inferior products, using high-pressure sales tactics, and cutting corners on installation to offer a cheaper price. We knew there was a better way.

Our mission is to be the antithesis of that model. We leverage our 360° expertise to provide a transparent, educational, and uncompromisingly high-quality experience. We are the team you hire when you want the job done right, once.
